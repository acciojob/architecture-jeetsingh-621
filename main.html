Architectural Wonders Around the World

The Eiffel Tower in Paris, France, is one of the most iconic landmarks. Completed in 1889, it is made of iron and stands 324 meters tall. Designed by Gustave Eiffel, it was the tallest structure in the world until 1930.
The Burj Khalifa in Dubai, UAE, holds the title of the tallest building in the world at 828 meters. Its sleek and modern design is inspired by Islamic architecture, and it serves as a mixed-use skyscraper with residences, offices, and a hotel.
